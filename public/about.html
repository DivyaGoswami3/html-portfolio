My journey as a developer

My name is Divya Goswami, and I am a passionate web developer with a love for creating dynamic and user-friendly websites. My journey in web development began in 2025, when I first discovered the power of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Over the years, I have honed my skills and expanded my knowledge in various web technologies. I have worked on numerous projects, ranging from simple static websites to complex web applications. When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing my knowledge with the developer community. I am always eager to learn and grow as a developer, and I look forward to the exciting challenges that lie ahead.
